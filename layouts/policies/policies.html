Housing: Homes for Locals

Cornwall's housing crisis demands bold action. We champion restrictions on second-home ownership, genuinely affordable social housing on public land, and policies that ensure Cornwall remains a place where local people can afford to live — not just visit.

{{/* Local Economy — dark card */}}
payments

Local Economy

Supporting independent Cornish businesses through procurement policies that keep money circulating locally, pushing back against extractive business models, and championing sustainable economic growth rooted in our communities.

{{/* Environment — image card */}}

Environmental Stewardship

Protecting Cornwall's coastline, rivers, and countryside. We advocate for stronger protections against pollution, responsible development, and conservation that prioritises our natural heritage for future generations.

{{/* Transport — split card */}}

Integrated Transport

Cornwall needs a connected, affordable transport network. We support better bus connectivity between rural communities and urban centres, investment in rail services, and infrastructure for electric vehicles.
