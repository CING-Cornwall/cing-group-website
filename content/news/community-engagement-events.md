CING councillors are committed to being visible and accessible in the communities they represent. Over the coming weeks, several of our members will be holding drop-in sessions and community meetings across the county, and we warmly invite residents to attend.

These events are an opportunity to raise local concerns directly with your councillor, hear about current council business, and find out how CING is working on the issues that matter to you. Whether it is a pothole on your road, a planning application in your neighbourhood, or a broader question about council policy, no issue is too small or too large to bring along. Our councillors are there to listen, and wherever possible, to act.

Details of specific dates, times, and venues will be published on this page and shared through our social media channels as they are confirmed. If you would like to suggest a venue or topic for a future event in your area, please get in touch through the contact form on our Get Involved page. Community engagement is not a one-off exercise for us — it is the foundation of everything we do.
