Councillor Anna Thomason-Kenyon has been elected as Vice-Chair of the Falmouth Community Area Partnership (CAP), marking a positive step forward for collaborative working and local engagement in the area.

The Falmouth CAP brings together Cornwall Councillors, town and parish councils, community organisations, and key stakeholders to identify priorities, shape local investment, and deliver practical improvements for residents.

Speaking following her appointment, Cllr Thomason-Kenyon said:

> "I'm honoured to have been elected as Vice-Chair of the Falmouth CAP. This is a real opportunity to strengthen collaboration across our communities and ensure local voices are at the heart of decision-making.
>
> I'm particularly keen to support projects that improve connectivity, protect our environment, and enhance the wellbeing of residents across Falmouth and the surrounding area."

Cllr Rowland O'Connor, Leader of the Cornish Independent NonAligned Group (CING), welcomed the appointment:

> "Anna's election is thoroughly deserved. She brings a strong commitment to community engagement and a practical, solutions-focused approach.
>
> I have no doubt she will play a vital role in ensuring the Falmouth CAP delivers meaningful outcomes for residents."
