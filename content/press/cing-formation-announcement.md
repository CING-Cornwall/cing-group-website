The Cornish Independent NonAligned Group (CING) is pleased to announce its formation as a new independent group on Cornwall Council.

CING brings together three councillors — Rowland O'Connor (St Columb Major, St Mawgan & St Wenn), Anna Thomason-Kenyon (Constantine, Mabe & Mawnan), and Karen Knight (Camborne West & Treswithian) — who have chosen to sit together as independent representatives, free from any national party whip.

## Our Commitment

CING councillors will vote on the merits of each issue, guided solely by the interests of Cornwall and the communities we represent. We are not a political party. We are a group of independents united by a shared commitment to:

- **Local accountability** — holding the administration to account through rigorous scrutiny
- **Transparent governance** — open, honest representation without party political games
- **Cornwall-first priorities** — focusing on housing, environment, local economy, and transport

## Group Leadership

Rowland O'Connor has been elected as Group Leader, with Anna Thomason-Kenyon serving as Deputy Leader. Between them, the three councillors sit on a wide range of committees including Audit, Standards, Constitution & Governance, Corporate Finance & Performance, Licensing, and Planning.
